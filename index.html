ABOUT US 

CAAPL(Custard Apple Animation Pvt.Ltd)in an independent game/application/web development company based in pune. we started in 2019 with a dream of making fun oriented games and user friendly applications.development is the keyword which defines us.Being a team full of young bloods we are high in energy,enthusiasm and have new outlook towards things. Same can be seen in the work done by us.

Our Mission
To offer billions of first time mobile gamers across emerging markets a curated, innovative mobile gaming service where they can play & have fun with their friends and make new friends and many more applications with different services.

Our Vision
Generate billions of smiles on mobile devices any time, any place across millions of people in emerging markets who in their real life are grappling with the *The Game of Life*.





SERVICES  

Mobile Games

Game development is something we excel in and really enjoy doing. We have a team of expert game developers, designers and artists who are capable of producing games which are as fun to play as much as they are great to look at. We are happy helping our customers build their next awesome game.

Web Designing

The latest buzz in the town is creating virtual identities; for people, places, organizations or institutions. People are coming up with web pages and sites for multifarious functions. In this era, where most things are available at the click of a mouse, it becomes important to have a representation on the web. We help create and design websites. We cater to different needs based on user preference.

Mobile Applications

Customized mobile app development has been a staple service of CAAPL since our inception. CAAPL has a robust team of programmers, designers and business advisors. On the technical side, we have a very experienced Team. On the graphics side, we have a highly competent creative team of designers and graphics artists. Also, we can add immense value to your project by giving you advice on improving your app idea and marketing it effectively to ensure visibility in the app marketplace.

Virtual Reality Applications and Games

Virtual reality development is best defined by art meets technology. When employed creatively, it can help businesses market products and services, engage with customers and drive sales in manners unrivaled by most other medium. The simplest description we can come up with to explain Virtual reality is the following technology which offers a perspective of a real-world environment where it is augmented by audio and visual inputs, in the process offering observers an immersive interactive experience.
